# About the Game

**8 Ball Bomb – Brick Breaker** delivers a fresh and explosive take on the legendary **brick breaker arcade genre**.

Aim carefully and launch a volley of powerful balls to destroy numbered bricks before they descend to the bottom of the screen. Every shot counts — miss your timing and the board quickly fills with bricks.

With smooth physics, satisfying explosions, and unpredictable power-ups, the game creates a fast-paced and addictive arcade experience.

---

# Core Gameplay

### 🎯 Aim, Shoot, Destroy

The bricks are constantly descending.

Your mission is simple:

- Aim carefully
- Shoot a volley of **8 powerful balls**
- Destroy numbered bricks
- Prevent them from reaching the bottom

The longer you survive, the harder the game becomes.

---

# ⚡ Random Power-Ups

Destroy bricks to unlock powerful bonuses that can instantly change the game.

**2X Damage**  
Smash bricks twice as fast and clear difficult walls.

**16 Ball Storm**  
Double your firepower and flood the screen with bouncing balls.

Power-ups appear randomly, creating unpredictable and exciting moments in every round.
